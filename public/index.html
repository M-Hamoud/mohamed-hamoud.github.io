I am a Swiss Finance Institute PhD candidate in Finance at the University of Zurich. My PhD advisor is Prof. Felix Kübler. I hold a Master’s degree in Economics and Bachelor degrees in Economics and in Mathematics.

My research interests include asset pricing, macro-finance, and monetary economics.
